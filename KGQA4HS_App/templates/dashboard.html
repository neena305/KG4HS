About Project

KGQA4HS is a domain-specific Knowledge Graph Question Answering system for Hindu Scriptures. It allows users to ask natural language questions and retrieve answers from structured graph data. The system covers Ramayana, Mahabharata, Vedas, and Bhagavad Gita, and presents results in the form of answer text, tabular output, and subgraph visualization for better interpretability.

Objective

To support explainable question answering over Hindu scripture knowledge graphs.

Technologies

Flask, Neo4j, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Vis Network.

Domains

Ramayana, Mahabharata, Vedas, and Bhagavad Gita.

Output

Answer, table, performance time, and graph-based reasoning path.
